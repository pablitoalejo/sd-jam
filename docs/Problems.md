## Abhishek 
Communication for me, in a design jam, is about creating meaning. It is not just about the product, service, or system. It is about understanding the core value of it and how it is being delivered. It is also important for everybody to understand that language.

One of the biggest inspirations is the Business Model Canvas designed by Alexander Osterwalder, which is a method used to create clarity and bring people onto the same page. It also helps us understand what we are working on and understand each other's language, because it is so easy to work with.

I feel like communication is also beyond language. It is emotion, culture, people's past experiences, and their future aspirations. Communication can be different and mean different things to different people. We have visual thinkers, auditory thinkers, and kinesthetic aesthetic learners, so I think the idea of rethinking human communication is to bring us to a point where we are all talking in our own agency that we are born with. We are also able to understand fluently what the other person is saying and meaning, and assign value to it.
